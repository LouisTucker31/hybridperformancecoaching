FAQs

Common questions about bespoke plans.

Can I keep lifting weights while training for a race?

Yes, that's the whole idea behind a hybrid plan. Your lifting sessions are built alongside your running or cycling volume so one supports the other instead of getting dropped.

How much does a bespoke plan cost?

The Bespoke Plan is £49, the Bespoke Plan + Consultation is £79, and the Performance Plan is £99. All three are one-off payments, not ongoing memberships.

Do I need previous gym experience to start?

No. Your plan is built around your current level, whether you're new to structured training or already competing.

Is there ongoing support after I get my plan?

Each package includes a defined window of email support after delivery rather than an open-ended membership, so you know exactly what's included.

Can you build a plan for a specific event, like a triathlon or a running race?

Yes. Whether it's a triathlon, a running race or a cycling event, the plan is built around your target date and structured to have you peaking at the right time.

Is this available remotely?

Yes, it's fully remote. Everything is delivered as a written plan and spreadsheet tracker you can follow from anywhere.
