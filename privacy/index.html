Privacy Policy

What I collect, why, and how you can ask me to delete it. Last updated: 17/07/2026.

What I collect

When you use the Contact form on this site, I collect the information you enter: your name, email address, and depending on what you share, your main goal, event date, current training, gym/cardio availability, any injuries or limitations, and your message.

Why I collect it

To respond to your enquiry, assess whether a bespoke plan is a good fit for you, and provide the service you've asked about. I don't use it for anything else, and I don't sell or share it with third parties for marketing.

Legal basis

You provide this information voluntarily by submitting a form, which counts as your consent. Where you share health-related details (like injuries), that's given with your explicit consent for the purpose of training safety.

Third parties

  • Form submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • This site loads the Inter font from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of that request.

How long I keep it

I keep your information only as long as needed to respond to you or deliver the service you've signed up for. If you'd like it deleted sooner, just email me.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your data, or object to how it's used. Email me at louistucker@live.co.uk and I'll action it.

Cookies

This site doesn't set its own cookies or run analytics. Google Fonts may involve minimal technical data as noted above.
