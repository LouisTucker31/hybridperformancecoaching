FAQs

Common questions about coaching.

Can I keep lifting weights while training for a race?

Yes, that's the whole point of hybrid coaching. Your gym sessions and your running or cycling are planned together, so lifting supports your endurance work instead of competing with it.

How much does a bespoke plan cost?

The Bespoke Plan is a one-off £49. Bespoke Plan + Consultation, which includes a call and 2 weeks of email support, is £79. The Performance Plan, built around a specific event with 4 weeks of email support, is £99.

Do I need previous gym experience to start?

No. The plan is built around your current gym and cardio availability, whatever that looks like right now, and adjusted as you go.

Is there ongoing support after I get my plan?

The Bespoke Plan includes one round of email revisions. The Consultation and Performance tiers include a defined window of email support (2 or 4 weeks) to sort out any issues, rather than ongoing weekly check-ins.

Can you build a plan for a specific event, like a triathlon or a running race?

Yes. Tell me your event and date on the form below and the plan, including taper and peak weeks, gets built around it.

Is this available remotely?

Yes, everything is delivered remotely via your spreadsheet tracker and email, so it works wherever you're training.
