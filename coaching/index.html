FAQs

Common questions about coaching.

Can I keep lifting weights while training for a race?

Yes, that's the whole point of hybrid coaching. Your gym sessions and your running or cycling are planned together, so lifting supports your endurance work instead of competing with it.

How much does 1-to-1 coaching cost?

Plan Only is a one-off £49. Standard coaching with weekly check-ins is £79/month, and Performance coaching with event-specific peaking is £99/month. Beta spaces get founder pricing, the lowest it will ever be.

Do I need previous gym experience to start?

No. The plan is built around your current gym and cardio availability, whatever that looks like right now, and adjusted as you go.

How does the weekly check-in work?

You'll get WhatsApp support and a weekly check-in to review how training went, adjust for fatigue or schedule changes, and keep the plan moving in the right direction.

Can you coach me for a specific event, like a triathlon or a running race?

Yes. Tell me your event and date on the form below and the plan, including taper and peak weeks, gets built around it.

Is coaching available remotely?

Yes, all coaching is delivered remotely via your spreadsheet tracker and WhatsApp, so it works wherever you're training.
