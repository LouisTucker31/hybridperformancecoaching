FAQs

Common questions about the plans.

What's included in each training plan?

Every plan comes as a PDF guide plus a spreadsheet tracker, covering weekly structure, session breakdowns, progression, substitutions and notes on fatigue and recovery.

Do I need a gym membership to follow these plans?

The plans are written around standard gym equipment, but exercises can be substituted depending on what you have access to.

Are these plans suitable for beginners?

Yes. The 5K Plan for Lifters is written specifically for beginners to running who already lift, and the other plans include guidance on scaling to your current level.

How do I get the plan after I buy it?

Plans are sold through Gumroad and delivered instantly as a download, no waiting.

Is there support included, or am I following it alone?

These plans are self-guided. If you want check-ins, adjustments and direct support alongside your plan, that's what 1-to-1 coaching is for.

Is there a triathlon training plan?

Yes, the First Sprint Triathlon plan is built for exactly that, with a race-week taper included.
