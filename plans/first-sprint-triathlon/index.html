FAQs

Common questions about this plan.

How is this different from a generic triathlon plan?

It's built specifically for beginners doing their first sprint distance, with three structured sessions a week rather than the higher volume most triathlon plans assume.

Do I need to already be a strong swimmer?

No. The plan is built for beginners, with swim sessions progressing gradually so you build confidence in the water alongside your bike and run.

Will three sessions a week be enough to finish?

Yes. One swim, one bike and one run a week, structured progressively over 12 weeks, is enough to get a beginner comfortably to the finish line of a sprint triathlon.

What if I don't have easy access to a pool, bike or open water?

The plan is written around standard gym pool and turbo trainer or road access, with notes on adapting sessions to what you have available.

What do I need to use the tracker?

The interactive training tracker works in either Google Sheets or Excel, whichever you already use, so there's nothing extra to set up.

Is there support if I get stuck?

This plan is self-guided. If you'd like check-ins and direct support alongside it, that's what 1-to-1 coaching is for.
